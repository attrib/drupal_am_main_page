DrupalCamp Frankfurt

13./14. April 2014

Frankfurt am Main, Germany

Drupal Camp

The first DrupalCamp in Frankfurt will be on the 13th and 14th of April 2014.

This camp is for everyone and will be a bilingual camp. All sessions for experts and intermediates will be in English. The beginner session will be in German, as we decided nobody from further away will probably interested in these,

We also want to provide a sprint room if you want to hack together with other Drupalers here in Frankfurt.

Currently we are working at our sponsor concept, but if you are interested contact us at info@drupal-am-main.de.

We hope we'll see you in Frankfurt!

Frankfurt

Frankfurt is located in the middle of Europe and is perfectly for a international Drupal Camp, as it has one of the best access to public transport. So it shouldn't be a Problem to join us here in Frankfurt. As of now Frankfurt is more popular for the stock exchange and banks, but we also want to exchange all of our Drupal wisdom! And for the evenings we can get together and try out the local "Ebbelwoi" in one of the many pubs here.
